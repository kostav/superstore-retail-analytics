- **Data Cleaning**: SQL-based integrity checks (nulls, duplicates, invalid ranges) and Python pipeline for schema normalization.
- **Regional Analysis** (`src/region_analysis.py`): Exploratory analysis of profit, sales and category composition across geographic regions.
- **Product Analysis** (`src/product_analysis.py`): Profitability, volatility (CV) and stability metrics at product & category level, including risk profiling for the assortment.
- **Customer Analysis** (`src/customer_analysis.py`): Behavioural profiling of new vs returning customers, LTV (lifetime value) estimation and distribution of profit across the customer base.
- **Correlation Analysis** (`src/correlation_analysis.py`): Exploratory analysis of relationships between variables such as discount, sales, profit, and category/region effects, including initial checks for non-linearity and multicollinearity.
